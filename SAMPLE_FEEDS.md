## Usage Tips

1. **Create a Category**: First, create categories like "Technology", "News", or "Science".
2. **Add a Feed**: Copy one of the URLs above and add it using "Add Feed".
3. **Select a Category**: Choose the appropriate category when adding the feed.
4. **Wait**: The first load may take a few seconds.
5. **Enjoy**: Read the articles and mark them as read!

## Note

Some RSS feeds may change or be removed over time. If a feed is not working, look for the RSS icon on the site's homepage or try suffixes like `/feed`, `/rss`, or `/feed.xml`.
